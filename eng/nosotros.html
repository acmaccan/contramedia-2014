CONTRAMEDIA FILMS is an audiovisual producer founded by Hernán Zin in October 2008. We have quickly and sustainably grown ever since.

We started our most ambitious project, 'War Against Women', in 2009 together with the American producer Island Bound, shot in over 10 countries for 3 years. In 2011, we started producing documentaries and programs for TVE (Spanish state TV) and Canal Plus.

In 2012, we expanded our services to publicity and training, working for clients like Warner, BBVA, Deloitte and the Spanish National Drama Center. In 2013, our productions reached channels like National Geographic.
