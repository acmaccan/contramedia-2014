We launched in 2013 the first Master's Degree in Documentary Production, Report and New Audiovisual Genres in the Universidad Europea de Madrid, supported by Discovery Channel and Sony.

The only goal of the Master's Degree is to attend the dramatic technological and narrative languages transformation when producing non-fiction movies.

The student will go deep along 500 hours in the new cameras handled by filmmakers. An especially revolutionized universe due to HDLR cameras.

The student will also discover new languages in docureality, docushow, docutainment, docudrama, docu-soap, fly on the wall, coaching TV and infotainment, by undertaking filming and editing daily projects.
