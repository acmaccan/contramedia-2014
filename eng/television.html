2010_

13 OBJECTS

SYNOPSIS
How could we bring international contents close to those who usually do not pay them attention for being too far from their reality? How to catch their attention on amazing issues that do not reach many people due to the geographical and cultural distance, such as war or weapons traffic? There is a simple answer: using objects close to the audience, that they have at their homes, that are part of their daily life and using these objects as a road to far issues. This is the logic behind the program '13 Objects', where an object, such as the slipper on the pilot program, will drive us from story to story until portraying a complex reality such as violence in Argentina.

CREDITS
Host & Director: Hernán Zin
Executive Producers: Diego Pignataro
Production: Contramedia Films
Format: 16/9 HD
Length: 54' (Pilot)
