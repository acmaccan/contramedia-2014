FLY TO TELL

SYNOPSIS

THE MIRACLE OF BLUES

SYNOPSIS
Documentary about the Cazorla Festival winning the award to the world's best blues festival, in Memphis, on its 20th anniversary. THE REPORTER'S SUITCASE

SYNOPSIS
This project produced by TVE and Contramedia Films approaches a topic that had not been shown on a documentary before -- the psychological consequences suffered by reporters working on war zones. From Spain and the rest of the world, master reporters tell their worst memories and explain how they deal with them. Moreover, we will get to know deeper the role of those who help journalists with PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder).
Each main character in 'The Reporter's Suitcase' will talk about a specific conflict, so we will also take a trip to the war, from Vietnam to nowadays.

CREDITS
Director: Hernán Zin
Producers Andrés Luque/Hernán Zin
Production: TVE/Contramedia Films
Format: 16/9 HD
Length: 55/82 min
Release: TVE

2014_

BEBE & YOU

SYNOPSIS
Bebe sets out on an acoustic tour around different stages in Latin America and Spain to celebrate the 10th anniversary of her first album, 'Pafuera Telarañas'. It is a look back to basics, when she started playing in bars around Madrid.
A close and unprecedented approach that will allow us to get to know Bebe better, on an off the stage, on flights, press conferences, hotel rooms, writing times, fan encounters and collaborations.
A personal portrait of a unique personality who has touched millions of people with her songs all over the world.

CREDITS
Director: Hernán Zin
Producer Hernán Zin
Script: Bebe
Production: Contramedia Films
Format: 16/9 HD
Length: 82 min
Release: Canal Plus

2013_

WAR AGAINST WOMEN

SYNOPSIS
It took 3 years shooting in 10 different countries and over 100 interviews in 20 different languages to give voice to women suffering sexual abuse in wars. A call of attention to the world's passive attitude regarding women as war weapons. Also a tribute to those women who do not give up, go ahead and raise their voices in order to keep these atrocities from happening.

CREDITS
Director: Hernán Zin
Executive Producer: Ana Picus
Script: Ana Picus, Hernán Zin
Production: Island Bound, Contramedia
Format: 16/9 HD
Length: 62 min

2013_

I WANT TO BE MESSI

SYNOPSIS
Argentina is one of the biggest soccer player factories worldwide. Tons of soccer stars came out of its schools and its streets to reach the top of the most popular sport in the planet. 'I Want To Be Messi' shows, through 3 different stories told first hand, how this awesome factory works, with its lights and shadows, its victories and broken toys. These stories are alternated with testimonies by coaches, headhunters, agents, executives and parents.

CREDITS
Director: Hernán Zin
Executive Producer: Andrés Luque/Fernando Pastini
Producer: TVE, Contramedia
Release: TVE
Broadcast: National Geographic, Swiss TV...
Format: 16/9 HD
Length: 82 min and 56 min
Awards: Research Espiello prize

SLUM WORLD

SYNOPSIS
Along the 20th century, humanity carried out the biggest migration in history. Millions of people left their lives in the country behind to move to the city. However, not all of them succeed. Over 1 billion people live in the suburbs of big cities, in marginal areas, set aside, known as slums or shantytowns.
